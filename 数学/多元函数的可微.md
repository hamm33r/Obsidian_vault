# 定义
>在点(x,y)处
## $$\Delta \text z=f(x+\Delta x,y+\Delta y)-f(x,y)$$$$\Delta \text z=dz+o(\rho)$$$$dz=\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\Delta x+\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\Delta y$$
>如果z可以写成$\Delta \text z=dz+o(\rho)$就说明z在这一点可微
>所以需要验证$o(\rho)是不是\rho$的高阶无穷小,也就是做**比值极限**,

### $$\rho=\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2}$$
所以
P315
1. 写$\Delta \text z=f(x+\Delta x,y+\Delta y)-f(x,y)$
2. 写$dz=\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\Delta x+\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\Delta y$
3. 写$o(\rho)=\Delta z - dz$
4. 求极限$\frac{o(\rho)}{\rho}=0 \rightarrow z在(x,y)可微$
