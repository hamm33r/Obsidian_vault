# 乘积求导公式的逆用
1. $$见到f(x)f'(x),令F(x)=f^2(x)$$
2. $$见到[f'(x)]^2+f(x)f''(x),令F(x)=f(x)f'(x)$$
3. $$见到f'(x)+f(x)\phi '(x),令F(x)=f(x)e^{\phi(x)}$$
# 商的求导公式的逆用
1. $$见到f'(x)x-f(x)，令F(x)= \frac{f(x)}{x}$$
2. $$见到f''(x)f(x)-[f'(x)]^2,令F(x)=\frac{f'(x)}{f(x)}\quad 或F(x)=lnf(x)$$
3. $$见到\frac{f'(x)}{f(x)},令F(x)=lnf(x)$$
