# 指数/对数函数求导 $$ (a^x)' = a^x \ln a \quad (a>0,a\neq1) $$ $$ (\log_a x)' = \frac{1}{x\ln a} \quad (a>0,a\neq1) $$ $$ (\ln|x|)' = \frac{1}{x} \quad (\text{遇到}\ln|x|\text{，不用分类讨论，求导都是}\frac{1}{x}) $$ 
---
## 反三角函数求导 $$ (\arcsin x)' = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} $$ $$ (\arccos x)' = -\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} $$ $$ (\arctan x)' = \frac{1}{1+x^2} $$ $$ (\text{arccot }x)' = -\frac{1}{1+x^2} $$ 
---
## 三角函数求导 
### 基础三角求导 $$ (\tan x)' = \sec^2 x $$ $$ (\cot x)' = -\csc^2 x $$ $$ (\sec x)' = \sec x \tan x $$ $$ (\csc x)' = -\csc x \cot x $$ 
### 补充恒等式 $$ \begin{cases} \sec^2 x = 1+\tan^2 x \\ \sec x = \frac{1}{\cos x} \end{cases} $$

## 对数型复合函数求导 $$ \left[\ln\left(x+\sqrt{x^2+1}\right)\right]' = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+1}} $$ $$ \left[\ln\left(x+\sqrt{x^2-1}\right)\right]' = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2-1}} $$
